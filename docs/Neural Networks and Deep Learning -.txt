Neural Networks and Deep Learning - My Notes

Neural networks are computing systems inspired by biological neural networks in animal brains.
They consist of layers of interconnected nodes called neurons.

Deep learning refers to neural networks with many layers, called deep neural networks.
The more layers, the more abstract features the model can learn.

Transformers are a type of deep learning model introduced in 2017.
They use a mechanism called attention to weigh the importance of different parts of the input.
Transformers are the foundation of modern LLMs like GPT and Gemini.

RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation.
It combines a retrieval system with a language model.
The retrieval system fetches relevant documents, and the LLM generates an answer based on them.
RAG reduces hallucination because the model is grounded in real retrieved content.

ChromaDB is a vector database used to store embeddings.
Embeddings are numerical representations of text that capture semantic meaning.
Similar texts have embeddings that are close together in vector space.
